FRS

The Partition MMS includes the merchant, merchant account, MID, and bank settings. This also includes a currency converter tool and the ability to see relationship between merchants and their accounts.


Submenu

Tree

Section Tree helps to see the full architecture between merchants and their accounts. You are able to watch which accounts are assigned to merchants or see the status of the accounts and the merchants.

Account

The merchant account in the Admin panel connects the merchant and the MID (acquiring bank account).

Bank

The Bank in the Admin Panel is an integrated bank or financial institution that processes credit or debit card payments on behalf of a merchant. The term acquirer indicates that the merchant accepts or acquires credit card payments from card-issuing banks within the association. The best-known credit card associations are Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express, Diners Club, Japan Credit Bureau and China UnionPay.

Get more details


Mid

The MID is defined in an agreement between a merchant and an acquiring bank for the settlement of payment card transactions.

Get more details


Merchant

Every payment made with a credit card involves the transfer of funds to a merchant account that a merchant has at a bank. The merchant has full responsibility for the transactions that occur with their account and each bank has its own terms of service to which account-holders must adhere.

Currency converter

Currency converter software is designed to check the value of one currency with another and convert it. The exchange rate is based on the current market or bank exchange rates.
